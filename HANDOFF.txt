KANA TRAINER (kana.html) — STATE
Navigable single-file app: Home menu → sections, with back-nav.
 ✅ Intro — hiragana vs katakana + how the kana system works + how to use.
 ✅ Learn — SRS flashcards: introduces 5 at a time by gojūon row; self-grade into 4 buckets
    (Know it / Kinda / Don't really / No clue); a row unlocks the next when every kana in it is
    "consistently Know it" (lvl 3 + streak≥2); mixes in-order frontier drilling (~35%) with
    weighted-random across all learned kana; localStorage progress per script; 🔊 Web-Speech TTS.
 ✅ Write — finger-tracing over a faint guide char on a canvas (clear / hide-guide / next).
 ✅ Sounds & Extras — teaching + tap-to-check practicelets: dakuten ゛/handakuten ゜(voiced + bilabial p),
    yōon combos (small ゃゅょ), long vowels (おう/ー), small っ (sokuon double consonant).
 ✅ Mnemonic IMAGE shown on the flashcard flip when available.
 46 hiragana + 46 katakana, each with an original TEXT mnemonic.
