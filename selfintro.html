The blanks — make it your own

名前 / なまえ — your name (foreign names are often written in katakana).

学年 / 職業 — your grade or occupation: 高校生 (high schooler), 大学生 (college student), 会社員 (office worker), 先生 (teacher)…

才 / さい — your age, any number (the app writes the kanji & reading for you).

一番好きな事 — your favorite thing about Japan so far. Pick something that shows your personality! (Use a dictionary or AI to find the word.)

More vocab: おんがく = music · たべもの = food · アニメ = anime · ゲーム = games · まんが = manga

Fill these in. Type names/words in kana if you can (or romaji/English is fine).
